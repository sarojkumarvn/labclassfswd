Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, fish) or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes. Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia, as well as among the diaspora from the region. Similar dishes are also prepared in other parts of the world such as in Iraq, Thailand, and Malaysia. Biryani is the single most-ordered dish on Indian online food ordering and delivery services.

Recipes of Different types of Biryani are available as follow:

1. Prawn Biryani:

  • Step 1 --> To prepare this Muslim recipe, take a large bowl mix chicken with yoghurt (curd), red chutney, and leave it for 30 min.
  • Step 2 --> Soak the Basmatic rice(250gm) in the water and spread some spices like Tej Patta, Laung(cloves), Bay leaf, etc. over it. Add 1.5 tbsp salt, and boil it for few minutes. When the rice is soften, drain.
  • Step 3 --> Once the above is well fried, shift into a large area, and add flavor. When done is settled, add 1-3 tbsp boil-fried-onion, 1-2 tsp of butter, and few drops of orange food color over it.
  • Step 4 --> Cover the pan tightly and cook on very low flame for 15 minutes, then remove from the flame and sprinkle remaining fried onion.
  • Step 5 --> Now, add 50 per cent cooked Basmati rice (or Above the Fist layer, add brown or fried onion, ghee and few drops (if you like it spicy, the chilli powder). Repeat the same for 3rd layer.
  • Step 6 --> Cover with a foil and cook in a high flame for 15 minutes. Then simmer on a low flame for 5 more minutes. Remove the .well/foil, and Biryani is ready, dish out the Biryani is a low plate.

2. Handi Chicken Biryani:

This recipe for handi chicken biryani makes 4-5 serving worth of biryani, and takes around 2 hours for the entire process.

  • 1.4 hours.
  • Step 1 --> To cook the rice bring sufficient water along with all the spices to a boil. Add the rice and mix well.
  • Step 2 --> Marinate the chicken for at least 20 minutes. Heat half the ghee and fry onions until crisp.
  • Step 3 --> Transfer the marinated chicken to a greased clay pot. Spread a layer of the half cooked rice over it.
  • Step 4 --> Sprinkle remaining fried onion over this rice, then spread another layer of chicken and repeat this process.
  • Step 5 --> Cover with a foil and cook in a high flame for 15 minutes. Then simmer on a low flame for 5 more minutes. Biryani is ready to serve!
