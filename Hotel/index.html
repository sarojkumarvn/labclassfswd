Bhubaneshwar Biryani House
Biryani is a traditional dish which is very popular among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with rice, aromatic spices and meat or sometimes vegetables, eggs and potatoes. Served in many variations by mixing local spices, rice and meat (chicken, mutton, beef, prawn, or fish). Different flavors have developed from region to region, influenced by the local tastes.
We offer different types of Biryani, available as follows:
  • Chicken Biryani
  • Mutton Biryani
  • Egg Biryani
  • Veg Biryani
  • Prawn Biryani
